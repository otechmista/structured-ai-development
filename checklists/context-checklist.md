# Context View Checklist

Use this checklist before moving from Context View to Container View.

## Problem

- [ ] The problem is specific.
- [ ] The problem avoids generic statements.
- [ ] The problem explains why the system exists.

## Users

- [ ] Human users are listed.
- [ ] External systems are listed.
- [ ] Integrations are listed.
- [ ] Missing actors have been considered.

## Goals and Non-Goals

- [ ] Goals are explicit.
- [ ] Goals are measurable enough to guide design.
- [ ] Non-goals are explicit.
- [ ] Scope boundaries are clear.

## Business Rules

- [ ] High-level business rules are listed.
- [ ] Rules are not invented by AI.
- [ ] Ambiguous rules are marked as questions.

## Constraints

- [ ] Technical constraints are listed.
- [ ] Business constraints are listed.
- [ ] Legal or compliance constraints are listed, if relevant.
- [ ] Operational constraints are listed.

## Readiness

- [ ] The Context View is clear enough to define architecture.
- [ ] Open questions are documented.
- [ ] Risks are documented.
